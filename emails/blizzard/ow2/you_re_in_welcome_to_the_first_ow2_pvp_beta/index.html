WELCOME TO THE
OVERWATCH 2 PVP BETA
You're in! Get ready to party on the objective because your chance to play the Overwatch® 2 PvP Beta for PC is here.

Choose to play one of your favourite reworked heroes or take the newest member of the team, Sojourn, for a spin. Combat your way across a slew of new maps, 5v5 multiplayer, and a new push mode before anyone else, and don't forget to let us know what you think!

To get started in the Overwatch 2 PvP Beta:
  • Download the Battle.net desktop app if you haven't already and launch it.
  • Log in using the Battle.net account that you used to sign-up for Beta access.
  • Click the Overwatch icon located at the top of the launcher homepage.
  • Navigate to the Game Version drop-down menu located at the bottom left of the Overwatch game page.
  • Select Overwatch 2 Tech Beta, install, and press the blue Play button to start.*
The Overwatch 2 PvP Beta begins 26 April and is scheduled to run through 17 May.** Don't miss out on your opportunity to help shape the future of Overwatch!
Gather Intel
*Overwatch 1 on PC will be offered to all players as a free trial through Battle.net during the beta period. The free trial access to Overwatch 1 will end on 17 May (or other date which the Overwatch 2 Tech Beta ends).

**Dates subject to change based on testing needs.
COMMAND YOUR NEXT
OPERATION AS SOJOURN
Your next mission is to run point on the field as Vivian Chase—callsign: Sojourn—the 33rd hero to join the Overwatch hero roster. Lead your team to victory the only way a former Captain of Overwatch and Canadian Special Forces soldier knows how—with extreme mobility, tactful accuracy, and a fully upgraded cybernetic body to boot!

Ensnare your enemies with a Disruptor Shot before blasting them with the built-up energy from Sojourn's Railgun. Gain the high ground with her rocket-powered Power Slide that can be canceled into a high leap at any time, before initiating Overclock—a powerful ultimate ability that automatically charges Sojourn's Railgun abilities with unrelenting force. Keep your eyes on the objective and do whatever it takes to bring your whole team home, captain!
